## How to Choose and Set Up Your E-Commerce Hosting

Ready to launch? Here's my step-by-step from scratch took me under an hour with Bluehost.

1. **Assess Needs**: Low traffic? *Shared hosting*. Growing? *VPS hosting*. Use tools like BuiltWith to check competitors.
2. **Compare Plans**: Look for *free SSL*, *daily backups*, and *customer support* trials. Read U.S.-focused reviews on Trustpilot.
3. **Sign Up and Migrate**: Grab a *domain name* (often free year 1), then use their *site migration* tool export from old host via FTP.
4. **Install Platform**: One-click *WooCommerce* or *Shopify* import. Add *SSL certificate* and test *payment gateway integration*.
5. **Optimize Performance**: Enable *CDN*, compress images, and monitor with Google Analytics.
6. **Secure and Backup**: Activate *malware protection*, set auto-*daily backups*, and test restores.

**Pro Tip**: Start with a 30–97 day money-back guarantee to test real-world *performance*.
